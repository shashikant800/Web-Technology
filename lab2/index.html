Welcome to National Bird of India "Peacock"

India's national bird is the Indian Peacock (Pavo cristatus). Revered in Indian culture, the peacock symbolizes grace, beauty, and dignity. Known for its vibrant and iridescent plumage, the male peacock's elaborate tail feathers are adorned with eye-catching patterns in blue and green hues. In Hindu mythology, the peacock is associated with various deities, particularly Saraswati, the goddess of wisdom, and Kartikeya, the god of war. The peacock's dance during the monsoon is celebrated as a sign of joy and renewal.
